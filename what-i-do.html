What I Do

I focus on the challenge of connecting organisational silos.

Facilitating Coaching Advising Consortiums

Facilitating

I run workshops on the topic of collaborative working and transformational change as part of leadership development programmes or other educational programmes.

Coaching

I often act as an organisational coach, sharing the lessons learned from my years of practical experience, in order to help my clients to save time and effort and avoid common pitfalls. I work with leaders and also with the change agents who influence and support these leaders.
